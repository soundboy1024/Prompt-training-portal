Operational Controls and Menus

Front Panel Controls

The front panel features a high-resolution LCD screen, **Rotary Encoder** for navigation, and dedicated **HOME**, **BACK**, and **MUTE** buttons. It also includes I/O meters and a headphone jack.

Menu Navigation

The menu is logically organized, with a **Home Screen** for an overview and a **Main Menu** for detailed control of **INPUTS**, **OUTPUTS**, **ROUTING**, **SYSTEM**, and **SETUP**.

Troubleshooting

No Audio Output

Check gain settings and ensure the output is not muted.

Distorted Audio

Reduce input gain to prevent signal clipping.

No Dante Connection

Verify the Ethernet cable is connected and the link LEDs are on.

Block Diagram

The DSP's signal flow moves from left-to-right:

  • **Inputs:** Analog and Digital inputs enter the system.
  • **Processing Core:** Signals are processed and routed.
  • **Outputs:** Processed signals are sent to the final outputs.

Block Diagram: Audio Signal Path

Inputs

Analog signals are converted to digital. Digital signals from the Expansion Card go directly to the DSP Core.

Processing Core

This central block handles channel processing (**Gain, EQ, Dynamics, Delay**) and a **Mixing Matrix** that routes inputs to all 32 busses.

Outputs

Audio is converted back to analog or sent digitally via AES to the XLR outputs.
