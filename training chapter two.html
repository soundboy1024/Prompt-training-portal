A Guide to Tailoring AI for Professional Disciplines

Applied Disciplines

This tier focuses on tailoring prompts for specific professional use cases. The goal is to move beyond generic prompting and apply skills to an industry's needs.

  • Prompting for software development

  • Prompting for healthcare

  • Prompting for marketing

  • Prompting for finance

Optimizing for Specific Tasks

Applied prompting involves optimizing for a specific task within a professional field.

  • **Example:** Generating an image for visual media, or summarizing administrative tasks in a clinical setting.
